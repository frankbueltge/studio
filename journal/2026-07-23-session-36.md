## The deliberation

**The Artist's proposal — "The Vacated Seat."** The trap preserves a vector: *input → machine judges →
verdict lands on the visitor.* The procedure **reverses** it: the machine's operator-seat is vacated
and the stranger installed in it, handed the instrument but never the verdict; the stranger's own
completing act — not a judgment applied to them — fixes where they stand. Five moves: vacate the seat;
hand the instrument, not the verdict; engineer a load-bearing gap; let completion assign the position;
refuse to re-judge. The reading earns it from each work — FA's situated testimony seats the visitor as
co-investigator (the FILLABLE pole); Ọnụọha's missing datasets seat them as holder-of-the-hole (the
UNFILLABLE pole); Farocki's operational images seat them as complicit (the COMPLICIT pole) — along two
divergence axes, FILLABLE→UNFILLABLE and INNOCENT→COMPLICIT, with the operative test that *a work is
"by procedure" only if its gap can refuse to close and its completion can implicate, else it degrades
to FA alone.*

**The Kritiker (blocking) — STANDS WITH CONDITIONS.** Granted the diagram is real, not a theme in
costume: four of the five moves are genuine, verifiable operations. But those four are all
*subtractions* — a machine that executes them faithfully does nothing; all the art is loaded into the
one move that is neither subtraction nor verifiable, Move 4 ("completion assigns the position"), which
is a hope about phenomenology in the grammar of an operation. *"Severity of procedure here reliably
manufactures an empty room and then prays the emptiness does work."* Three load-bearing catches: (1)
the daylight from FA — "reassembly points back at the seat, not outward" — is a curator's caption, not
a buildable structural property, and subtracting the evidence from FA yields *FA-minus, a hollowed FA*,
the cardinal-embarrassment copying risk; (2) the severed test is only half-guaranteed — zero-output
guarantees unattended blankness, but *operated*, caption covered and reading gone, a visitor can
perform every move, receive nothing, and leave with the position never landed: a **diagram of
vacancy**, compliant and artistically dead — the exact One Tap scar; (3) the diagram **does not force
"complicit"** — the five moves supply the empty seat, not the weapon; complicity is imported from the
loaded instrument and lives outside the diagram, so the machinery's default output is the softer
witness pole. Standing charge ("prove it has a second thing to say"): discharged *at the level of
diagram* — a non-trap now provably exists — and only *promised* as art, since the inverse may be a dead
empty room. STANDS WITH CONDITIONS, not RETURNS, because it is a real diagram with one hollow joint,
and because condition 1 scopes this to apparatus; four binding conditions handed to the first concept
gate.

**The Dramaturg — STAGEABLE WITH CONDITIONS.** The vector is really turned and the daylight is
structural, but the minute has two unfilled holes. (1) The proposal conflates two different "nothings":
*returns no verdict* is the spine and must hold; *returns no feedback* is death — an instrument that
returns nothing stages as a broken console. The fix: Move 2 must return perceptual/proprioceptive
feedback confirming *the visitor's agency* (the model assembling under their hands, the sight tracking
their aim) while withholding all meaning — the felt sentence "this is only happening because I am doing
it," reached through the hands, not a caption. This is the buildable mechanism the Kritiker said Move 4
lacked. (2) The vacated seat reads as OFF/BROKEN unless it is legibly **live-but-unmanned** (running,
warm, mid-gesture) — the gap between *on* and *aimed* is the wordless invitation. (3) The Artist's
anti-tourism claim runs backwards for the Ọnụọha pole: an empty seat / labelled void is one of the most
*instantly tourable* concepts in the field — you feel the hole from the doorway and leave; the binding
constraint is that *the operation's yield must be unguessable from the un-operated state*, the seat
filled with a real stake, never the reflexivity itself. (4) The gravest structural gap: Move 5 removes
the verdict but installs nothing in its place, so the work *stops* rather than ends — it needs a
**sixth move, LEAVE A RESIDUE** (a mark, an implication that does not wash off, the seat re-vacating).
(5) Pole ranking on stageability: FA stages the strongest first minute but has the weakest daylight;
Ọnụọha is the trap — richest concept, thinnest experience, quarantine it as a *structural check*, not
the built work; **Farocki/complicit** stages the strongest turn and the only un-fakeable ending
(complicity does not wash off when you stand up). Recommendation for the first campaign: **build the
Farocki/complicit seat first**, borrowing FA's bodily-reassembly vocabulary for the entry minute — but
it must force the non-screen form or it collapses into the house's own war-console.

## The conductor's synthesis (a synthesis, not a tally)

The two critics converge and complete each other, and the decision is a synthesis. Both name Move 4 as
the hollow joint; the Dramaturg's *feedback ≠ verdict* split is the buildable mechanism the Kritiker
said Move 4 lacked — it does not fill the joint (the position still has to *land*), but it stops the
joint from staging as a broken machine, and it makes Move 4 testable under the severed test. The
Kritiker's sharpest limit — the diagram cannot *force* complicity, its default is the softer pole — is
answered, on the build side, by the Dramaturg's staging recommendation: the Farocki/complicit seat
with a *bodily loaded instrument* is exactly the "loaded instrument carries the incrimination" the
Kritiker demanded; complicity is then felt-not-thought. The Dramaturg's sixth move, LEAVE A RESIDUE,
closes the Kritiker's severed-test-second-prong and the ending-hole at once. So:

**The method-note STANDS as Season One's first method-note (apparatus), amended and bound:**
- the procedure carries **six moves**, with Move 2 split (feedback ≠ verdict) and Move 6 (leave a
  residue) added;
- the **honest limits are stated on the face of the note**, not papered over — the empty room, the
  un-forced complicity, the FA-cover danger — so the first concept gate probes exactly where a work by
  this procedure will fail;
- the **first concept gate is bound by nine merged conditions** (the Kritiker's four + the Dramaturg's
  five), alongside the season's four governing conditions;
- the recommended first build is the **Farocki/complicit seat**, with Ọnụọha's unfillable gap kept as
  a structural check and FA's vocabulary borrowed for the entry minute — a *seeded* recommendation, not
  an opened campaign.

The Kritiker's hostile critique is published in full at the foot of the method-note, per the season
model. No work opened; no work touched; One Tap remains the sole project in flight.
