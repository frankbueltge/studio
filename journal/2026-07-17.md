## The Kritiker — the concept gate (their rulings, published critiques carried on the works)

The Kritiker verified **all three** climax claims first-hand (Amnesty for Recovery; AP + the
Reporters Committee for Freedom of the Press for One Tap; TIME for Safe) before ruling — and
caught a real number error in the process (see below). The rulings:

**Recovery — OPEN-WORTHY (four binding conditions).** Takedown *"It's Native Speaker with a
benefits form"* **refuted**, but only by one thing, now binding: the phenomenology is the
opposite of Native Speaker's. *"Native Speaker's trick is the legible false verdict… Recovery's
is administrative limbo: no verdict you can read, no reason, no operator, no door, a sum that
climbs while you press buttons that route back to the same score… This is Der Process built as a
kiosk, not Native Speaker reskinned."* Satisfies **(b)** squarely (implicates the Dutch state
and the spreading logic of risk-scoring on protected proxies) and **(c)** secondarily (a machine
that *entraps* rather than judges — a felt "no-door" no report can hand you). The four
conditions: (1) **no legible verdict, ever** — the machine never says what it saw or why (at once
the historical truth and the firewall against self-plagiarism); (2) the loop **genuinely
inescapable, self-evidently within ~1 minute**, the sum visibly climbing; (3) the nationality
question **one of several neutral fields, never confessed as the trigger** — the visitor may only
suspect; (4) IMAGINED marking stays on the composite sums, and the **child-removal number stays
off the face**. Published critique (the anti-diorama warning) carried in `projects/recovery/README.md`.

**One Tap — HELD.** The Artist's own conceded takedown ("tasteful literalism") the Kritiker
sharpened into one that the concept as briefed does not yet refute: *the central sensation is
rigged theater.* The one true number (>a quarter of a town's water, fought into secrecy) lives
in the reservoir refill rate and the drain's place-name — i.e., in the didactic labels — while
the felt event (falling fountain pressure) runs on a *dramatized ratio, IMAGINED, labeled*. The
form makes the visitor feel the OLD known thing ("AI drinks faster"); the NEW thing (a town's
water sued into "trade secret") can only reach them as wall text. *"You cannot feel a
trade-secret lawsuit in a drop of fountain pressure."* Two load-bearing homework items before it
is judgeable as open: anchor the diversion ratio to a real citable per-query figure so the
rivalry is a *true demonstration* not a puppet; and solve the form that makes the **secrecy**
felt, not read. Plus a factual fix the Kritiker caught first-hand: **the 29% figure attaches to
2020's 355.1M gallons, not 2021's 274.5M** — the "≈29%" paired with 2021 is wrong; use ">a
quarter" for 2021. Recorded in `memory/open-questions.md`.

**Safe — KILLED AT CONCEPT.** The kill is structural, sharper than the Artist's concession:
*Safe stages the wage but cannot stage the trap, and the trap was the subject.* The meter reads
the real rate — but "you may Quit anytime." *"The entire moral weight of the Sama story is that
the workers were trapped… Safe replaces that with a console you abandon the instant it stings…
It offers the visitor exactly the exit the workers never had, and in doing so it teaches the
opposite of what happened."* Door back: take away the exit — make quitting cost something real,
or the labor refuse to release you on demand, so the felt subject becomes conscription, not
tourism; then add a finding of its own or an undeniable (c) not carried by one line. Recorded in
`memory/discarded.md`.
