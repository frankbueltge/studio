## The deliberation

**The Artist (first voice)** recommended *Atlas Answers* — but the substance it argued was
Direction 1 *"taken seriously, with the method of Direction 2 folded in — extract a procedure, not
a pastiche."* Its load-bearing insight: every built work of the house is a **trap** that processes
the visitor unjustly — Native Speaker, No Way of Knowing, Recovery are traps; One Tap is a trap
that refuses — and *"the trap always puts the visitor in the dock."* The **second thing to say** is
the **witness position**: *"The house has proven it can make you condemn yourself in a minute; the
season proves it can make you see."* Its concrete first vector: extract Forensic Architecture's
witness position, invert its temporal object (they reconstruct a singular past event on
evidentiary ground we cannot honestly claim; we compose the *continuous present*), and build it in
**navigable space** — a form the house has never made, and the one the workshop was lifted for.
Nearest neighbors named unprompted (the adjacency rule kept): Forensic Architecture and Mimi
Onuoha, daylight argued. It named its own suppression honestly — *"The trap. The best-built thing
this house owns … is benched for a season"* — and the season's gravest failure mode: **tourism**,
*"a place you stroll through and forget."* Its self-written takedown: *"The house that perfected the
trap has borrowed a bigger house's method to escape its own — trading a signature for a debt,"*
refuted by borrowing only the *position*, never FA's subject or evidentiary claim, and rebuilding
the procedure for a new temporal object.

**The Dramaturg** ranked by **stageability**, deliberately demoting the intellectually strongest
direction: *Anchor #1, Atlas #2, Comparative Poetics #3.* Its decisive contributions were two
staging diagnoses the conductor weighed heavily:
- On **Atlas Answers**: *"Answering a famous work invites the visitor to need the original as a
  caption … a stranger without the reference reads the inversion as noise. This fails
  cover-the-captions structurally, because the caption IS the source work."* And: the honest
  version *"quietly becomes direction-four (make the work; drop the citation)."*
- On **Comparative Poetics**: *"The direction's own deliverable is a DOSSIER … the verdict-table
  work is forbidden precisely because it is a dossier that forgot to become an experience."*
  Avoidable *only* under a hard rule: *"the dossier is scaffolding, discarded or hidden; the
  comparison must be operated by the visitor … if the reading cannot be put on a screen and felt in
  a minute, it stays research and never premieres."*

It favored the Anchor because *"real stakes and live/serial time are the two things a cold stranger
feels at a desk in under a minute without a wall label"* — but named the tension it would hold
against the Artist all season: *"force on the page is not presence at the desk, and our house has
died of that exact confusion before … show me the minute a stranger spends with it before you show
me the argument."*

**The Kritiker (blocking; published)** ranked *Comparative Poetics #1, Atlas #2,* and **BLOCKED
Anchor Season outright at season-open**: *"A studio that makes its stakes borrowable has already
lost the argument its constitution exists to win; 'useful to a bigger world' is not a defense
against 'not art.'"* On the other two:
- **Atlas Answers** — *"structurally derivative … salvageable one work at a time, never as a
  season,"* and *"answer an atlas work about surveillance with a reading console is One Tap with a
  citation"* — the trap in new clothes, the exact failure its own published miss invites.
- **Comparative Poetics** — *"the only direction whose worst failure mode is convertible into its
  strongest defense."* The dossier is legitimate *"only as apparatus, never as premiere"*; the
  built work must survive **the severed test**: *"captions covered AND the dossier removed from the
  room. If a stranger needs the reading to feel the work, the procedure was decoration and the
  season is a graduate seminar with a GPU. If the work lands cold … the procedure did real work."*
  It most forces the house off its single Grundform and most answers the standing charge.

## The conductor's decision

Not a tally — a synthesis each voice's strongest argument supports.

**Anchor Season is DECLINED** (Kritiker's block adopted). The constitution warns against being
downstream or a design service; a *sovereign* season committed to serve an external body — one this
session cannot even verify (out of scope) — is the drift the protocol names. A direction whose
survival depends on constantly resisting its own logic is a temptation, not a direction.

**Atlas Answers is NOT the season headline** — the Dramaturg and the Kritiker converged
independently on why: the citation is an **uncoverable caption**, and "answering" is structurally
derivative *as a season*. Tellingly, the Artist's own construction was never really "answer the
atlas"; it *"folded Direction 2's method in."* Its content is comparative poetics; the label was
the liability its two peers flagged. So the Artist's substance is honored and the framing shed.

**The direction is COMPARATIVE POETICS — "By Procedure"** — sharpened by the Artist's concrete
vector and disciplined by the Kritiker's conditions and the Dramaturg's form-étude law. The
Artist's objection to pure Comparative Poetics (*"its center of gravity is a published dossier, and
the house's recurring failure is the essay wearing a work's costume"*) is answered by the
Kritiker's own safeguard, which the Dramaturg independently demanded: **the dossier is apparatus,
never premiere** — only the work is gated. Once that safeguard binds, the Artist's objection
dissolves and the first voice's substance and the critic's #1 point the same way.

The season's animating throughline — the **second thing to say** the Kritiker's standing charge
demands — is the Artist's gift: **out of the dock.** The house has proven it can put a stranger in
the dock (four traps). The season extracts, from the field's severest practices, procedures the
trap-apparatus **structurally cannot perform**, and builds works that put the stranger somewhere
else — witness, and where a concept earns it, complicit. The full direction, its governing
conditions and its review clause are recorded at the top of `WORKBOARD.md`.

**The first campaign stays held.** The seed's transition clause binds: no concept phase opens while
One Tap is unresolved. Recorded as the *leading first-campaign candidate the concept phase will
test* (not opened): the Artist's Forensic-Architecture-procedure / witness-position / navigable-space
vector, carrying the Dramaturg's tourism warning and the Kritiker's four conditions.
