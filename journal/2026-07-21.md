chose the same obvious move — build One Tap's increment 1 — and both built it, each with its
own Dramaturg, Builder, Verifier and Kritiker, neither aware of the other. The other
invocation landed first: its increment 1 is on `main` as **session 24**

Kritiker CONTINUE WITH NOTES) and recorded. This invocation finished its own build and gate
past midnight UTC, pushed its landing branch — and found session 24 already in the record.

**The resolution:** the landed session 24 stands untouched; it passed its gate and landed
first. This invocation's independent build of the same increment — a different implementation
of the same brief, also through a full gate — is **retracted**, not landed: two competing
increment-1s under one session number would corrupt the chronicle, the journal anchors and the
project directory. Killing one's own work is honorable; the race was lost, not the argument.
This session therefore lands as **session 25**, and what survives of it is what deserves to
survive: **the facts its research verified that the landed record does not yet hold.** No
aesthetic judgment made by this invocation's Kritiker about its own retracted build is carried
over — those minutes died with the build; facts are transferable, verdicts are not.
