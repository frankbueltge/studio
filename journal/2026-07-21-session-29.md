## The Artist (strong tier): kill the instrument panel

The decisive move, quoted from its vision: *"Kill the instrument panel… I am not
decluttering it. I am re-composing it into a single gesture, staged in time, on the one
interface every stranger alive in 2026 already has a body-memory of: the prompt box you
type a question into."* The rejected alternative was named honestly — the "Recovery
treatment" (keep the dial, move the furniture behind the button) — and rejected for the
right reason: Recovery's face already trapped and only needed decluttering; here **"the
dial is the diagram."** The whole piece becomes: you ask; the answer that comes back is
what your question cost in water; and it will not form — not on a value, not even on a
size. The numeral resizes with magnitude (a near-invisible 0.26, a screen-filling 519):
the ~2,000× spread felt in the body of the type, not read off a scale.

Two more Artist decisions of consequence:

- **The stopwatch is cut entirely** — the last visitor-idle-time device. The session-27
  rhyme failed (Frank's ear is the empirical data) because its two halves had two subjects.
  The rhyme is re-founded on ONE subject: the withheld number, refused twice — yours by
  non-disclosure, the town's by litigation. Escalation, not echo.
- **Sound and the new workshop toolchain considered and DECLINED.** The verdict said
  over-instrumented; "the honest answer to that verdict is subtraction, not a new machinery
  layer… a shader that decorates is slop with a GPU." Typography is the protagonist. The
  restage thereby also answers the new Grundform rule: it escapes the
  screen-apparatus-with-visible-self-test register (dial, calibration voice, printed
  self-test all dead) rather than deepening it.

## The Dramaturg (strong tier): the staging plan

The governing law, which is also the tier grammar made structural: **IMAGINED motion is
ephemeral and owns the clock; the SOURCED record is persistent and owns the scroll.**
Viewport-locked beats — attract (a dark field, one box, "Ask it anything.") → thrash
(~10 s, five published values cycling at five magnitude-mapped sizes, owner and boundary
riding in a fixed sub-slot) → statement ("The cost of one question is not knowable…") →
resolution (~19 s to the core): the five come to rest as the still plate, five named
witnesses; from there down a permanent, re-readable record column — the cue ("But one
town's number — one figure, one year — took thirteen months in court to pry loose."),
the Dalles account in grave role-precise prose, the close, the identical box returned,
waiting. Nothing SOURCED ever vanishes; only the pre-resolution motion cannot be scrubbed
back — and should not be.

Notable staged decisions: **no attract-loop** — a deliberate anti-Recovery choice, argued:
the visitor typing their own real question IS the crossing; an auto-running thrash would
refuse no one. **The second ask is shorter and blunter** (~4 s, no re-teach, the record
persisting beneath) — "you asked differently, it refused identically, and now you know in
four seconds what took thirty-five the first time." The old placeholder caption ("type a
question… it goes nowhere") dies; its honesty moves into the one constant chrome line —
the tier law's constitutional minimum, Recovery's synthesis: *"An instrument, not a
service. The figures are real and published; nothing you type is sent or answered."*
Everything else — tier statements, all three numeric spreads, full pin scopes and sources,
the dated timeline with verbatim quotes, the fee-account disagreement, the labelled
residents COMMENTARY, the seed, the live self-test — behind "About this work," one press
away, still running every load.
