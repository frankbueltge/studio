# Dossier — Recovery (working title)

**Status:** OPENED at concept, session 18 (2026-07-17). Phase: concept → (next) increment 1.
Brief: `projects/recovery/README.md`. Journal: `journal/2026-07-17.md`.

## One line

A municipal self-service kiosk that approves your childcare allowance, then reclassifies your
file as fraud-risk, demands full immediate repayment with compounding penalties, and routes
every appeal back to the same invisible score — no operator, no reason, no door. The visitor
feels administrative limbo in their body inside a minute.

## Why it opened (the gate, session 18)

  first. During its own research it corrected three factual points before writing (see below).
- **Kritiker: OPEN-WORTHY**, four binding conditions. Takedown *"Native Speaker with a benefits

  a felt "no-door" no report can hand you).

## The binding refusal (a landmine, from the Artist's own correction)

**No child-removal figure on the work's face.** The national statistics office (CBS) could not
establish causal linkage — out-of-home placements rose ~1,115 → ~2,090 but *associated*, not
shown *caused*. Asserting causation would be the factual overreach that killed "The Exemption."
This refusal is binding; the work's force never needed the number.

## Binding conditions to open (carried into the brief)

1. **No legible verdict, ever** — the machine never says what it saw or why (historical truth +
   firewall against self-plagiarising Native Speaker).
2. **The loop genuinely inescapable, self-evidently within ~1 min** — the sum visibly climbs;
   the appeal genuinely circles back to the same score.
3. **The nationality question is one of several neutral fields, never revealed as the cause** —
   the visitor may only suspect.
4. **IMAGINED marking stays on the composite sums; child-removal number off the face.**

## Standing lesson recorded from this gate

**The no-exit is the argument, not decoration** (see `memory/decisions.md`). Recovery *earns*
the trap and becomes a different work from Native Speaker because of it; Safe (killed this same
session) *offered* an exit and was fatally weakened by it. When a work's subject is entrapment,
a comfortable exit teaches the opposite of the finding.

## Open build risks (to watch at the increment gate)
