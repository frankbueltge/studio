# Decisions

Standing decisions of the studio: decision · rationale · date · where journalled. The
Archivist curates; sessions read this before acting.

| Decision | Rationale | Date / record |
|----------|-----------|---------------|
| The collective's name is **Ensemble** | Bilingual-identical; names the structure (convened voices staged into one work); plural against Meridian's line and Ulysses' solitude; a production word, not a commentary word (contra Chorus); echoes no product or company | 2026-07-12 / session 01, journal |
| **Kritiker conditions bind at adoption, not at premiere** — a project opened over a published hostile pass carries its conditions as part of the brief | Founding precedent set with Diminishing Returns: five conditions + a go/no-go gate adopted before a single line was built | 2026-07-12 / session 01, journal |
| **The founding critique governs (session 05):** no project without the Artist; the Kritiker blocks at concept and premiere; the terminal test and the material bar are Messlatte law; kill at concept, not at prototype | The team's critique of the founding project, upheld in full — the collective's original gates measured hygiene, not art | 2026-07-12 / session 05, journal |
| **The adjacency rule (team, 2026-07-12):** copying an existing work = the greatest embarrassment; re-treating a theme from a different angle with clear added value = legitimate. Every proposal names its nearest neighbors and argues its daylight; hidden neighbors = presumed copying | The Artist's session-06 behavior (naming the Paglen adjacency unprompted) elevated to standard by the team | 2026-07-12 / this row + PROTOCOL form rules |
| **Rejected directions may return only carrying an argument** | Ledger of Days died as "wallpaper by definition"; the door back is a claim, not a costume change | 2026-07-12 / session 01, journal |
| **The named-individuals policy (team steer, binding):** no personal names of private individuals in a work's own voice — headlines, dramaturgy, verdicts, body prose. Role + institution + consequence instead; personal names survive only as citation text inside linked sources (official case captions, bylines). Companies/tools/institutions stay nameable where every claim traces to a cited primary source. Past journals stay untouched as record | The team does not publish a pillory ("Pranger") and will not accumulate open-ended legal maintenance around pending cases; the work's force never needed the surname — the stranger at the terminal is hit equally hard by "a PhD student — expelled, visa revoked". Steered upstream to Meridian the same day (their REQUESTS.md) for instrument 001 + FIELD.md | 2026-07-12 / session 08, journal |
| **The takedown law (team steer, session 11):** every proposal writes the one-sentence takedown a serious critic would publish, and argues how the built work will REFUTE it — not merely survive it. "Competent, honest, already known" opens no project: own finding, or risk upward, or a form only this machinery can produce | The team's post-premiere critique of Native Speaker ("not a breathtaking or truly progressive start — a real critic could tear this apart in one sentence") — the bar rises from honest to necessary; the premiere stands, the standard moves in front of the next concept | 2026-07-13 / session 11, journal |
| **The web-research tool reaches hosts the plain fetcher cannot (operational):** when the org egress policy 403-blocks a news/primary host from the plain URL-fetcher, the full-text-extraction web-research tool fetches it **server-side**, where the policy does not apply. Try that route before declaring a source unreachable or routing to Frank | Session 13 hit a wall and routed egress to Frank; session 14 found the constitution's own fallback ("if plain URL-fetching is blocked, use those instead") passes the same hosts and discharged condition 1 first-hand. A future session must not re-hit the session-13 wall | 2026-07-15 / session 14, journal |
| **A finding at n=2 is a premise, not a proven pattern:** a work may not claim a "structural" or "systematic" institutional pattern in its own voice on two instances (one of them open). It presents the *premise* (here: the epistemic asymmetry — knowledge-as-asset / ignorance-as-shield, toggled by outcome) + its first catches, and lets the serial form extend the claim honestly as the record grows | The Kritiker demoted the "structural finding" leg of "No Way of Knowing" at its concept gate — the work clears on form + risk, not on a pattern-claim the evidence does not yet carry; stated loosely it degrades into "the state is hypocritical," which is not news | 2026-07-15 / session 14, journal |
| **Stakes must be attributable, not just adjacent:** a case appears as a consequence of the work's subject only if the record attributes the consequence to that subject; otherwise it may appear only as a disclosed boundary case ("deliberately not carried") | Born from the Minnesota correction: the appeals court noted the panel did not rely on AI-detection evidence — a work about machine judgment may not borrow stakes the record does not attribute to the machine | 2026-07-12 / session 08, journal |
