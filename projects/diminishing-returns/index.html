Honesty panel

IMAGINED the framing, not a premise

"You cannot win" is not assumed anywhere in this piece — it is whatever roundVerdict() computes from the disclosed numbers below, every time it runs. If a future disclosed year shows growth at or below the winnable threshold, this same function declares that round winnable and the dial can win it. See the self-test section below, which proves this with synthetic numbers right now.

IMAGINED the dial-as-opponent, the rounds, the game's voice

The dial-as-opponent framing, the rounds-as-game structure, the effort resistance curve, and every word of this game's narrative voice are dramatization — a design choice made by this studio, not a fact asserted about the world or about Google.
