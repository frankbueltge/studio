## What it is

A two-faced console that can show only **one face at a time**. Face one is the state at maximal
confidence — the moment it *acts*: its own commanders, on the record, that machine/AI targeting
was "critical to our function" and let leaders "make smarter decisions faster than the enemy can
react," across thousands of targets. Face two is the same state at null — the moment it *harms*:
its verbatim answer to whether the machine was in the loop of a specific death — *"no way of
knowing."* The console will not show both. To read the second sentence you must lose the first.

The subject is **not** "AI killed this person" (unprovable; the console never claims it). It is
that the state said **both sentences on the record** — total epistemic confidence when it acts,
declared un-knowing when it harms — and the console makes a stranger *feel the seam* by forcing
the same either/or the state performs.

## Tiers

- **SOURCED / primary-confirmed** — the spine (both instances, all quotes verbatim from primaries;
  URLs in the dossier). Figures attributed per source, never harmonised.
- **IMAGINED** — the console's form and the "ACT / HARM" framing (marked as such on the work).

## The mechanism (condition 5 — designed, buildable)

Single full-bleed surface, one face at a time — **no tabs, no split view, no scroll containing
both.** Boots on **Face One (ACT)**: confidence sentences + target count + generic program
descriptor + date, verbatim, cited. Exactly one control, labelled with the state's own question:
**"Was the machine involved in this death?"** Pressing it is **destructive and slow (~2.5s)** —
Face One de-resolves to an empty field; only *from emptiness* does **Face Two (HARM)** assemble.
Never both legible at once; returning costs the same in reverse. The visitor **spends one sentence
to buy the other.** On-page self-test (house pattern) must prove the faces are never co-rendered.

**Anti-degradation guards (binding):** no persistent toggle, no back/forward that restores a face,
never co-rendered. The day the destruction becomes a cached animation over a preserved DOM, the
work is dead (the Spielerei takedown lands).

## The takedown, and how the built work refutes it

*Takedown:* "A competent, honest gallery restaging of a finding an investigative outlet already
published and closed — it juxtaposes two quotes anyone can read in the original source and adds no
fact, no risk, nothing new."

*Refutation (on (c) form + (b) risk — NOT on (a)):* only a serial, self-dating machine can hold a
**live, un-finalised** instance where the answer-slot honestly shows a pending question rather than
a written denial — terminal prose cannot, because the fact does not yet exist; and the destructive
switch produces a **finding of experience** (spend the boast to buy the denial) no article can. It
implicates a serving combatant command and the Pentagon by their own dated words in an
actively-investigated killing of children. The (a) "structural finding" is demoted per Kritiker
condition 1 — the work does not lean on it.
