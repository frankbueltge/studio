# Recovery (working title) — project brief

**Phase:** concept — **OPENED session 18 (2026-07-17)** through the concept gate
(Artist proposal + fought-for pick; Kritiker **OPEN-WORTHY** with four binding conditions;
climax claim verified first-hand twice — conductor and Kritiker independently). Zero Builder
tokens spent at open, per the kill-/open-at-concept law. Full record:
`memory/dossiers/recovery.md`, journal `2026-07-17.md`.

*(Working title only — "Recovery" carries the double sense the work turns on: the state's
**recovery** of the money it paid, set against the family's impossible **recovery** from an
accusation it can neither see nor answer. Revisable before premiere; the experience, not the
title, carries the terminal test.)*

## What it is

A single municipal self-service kiosk that ruins you by proxy and will not let you out —
because there is no human at the other end to let you out. You apply for a childcare
allowance. It approves you; money is "paid." Then, minutes later, the same screen
reclassifies your file — *risico geïndiceerd*, risk indicated — and demands you repay
everything, in full, immediately, with penalties, because a flagged file is presumed
fraudulent. You press **Contest**. It routes you: Verify Identity → Objection → back to the
same score. There is no operator, no reason given, no field where the machine will tell you
*what* it saw. The only thing that changes is the amount, which compounds while you stand
there.

The one-minute comprehension, in the body, not on a wall label: **I was fine, then a number
I cannot see decided I was a criminal, and there is no door.**

The subject is **not** "an algorithm made a mistake." It is the specific administrative
horror the real scandal ran on: not a legible false verdict, but *silence* — parents given
no reason, no appeal that reached a person, a debt that grew while they tried. The work makes
a stranger *feel the seam* between being scored and being told nothing.

## Daylight — the studio's own nearest neighbour first

The reddest adjacency flag is internal: the house has already premiered **Native Speaker**,
a machine that judges the visitor and ejects them. Recovery must prove it is a *different
work*, not the same trick reskinned — and it does, on phenomenology, not decoration:

- **Native Speaker** is the humiliation of a **legible false verdict** — the machine tells
  you who you are (a fake) and *spits you out*. Fast, terminal, readable.
- **Recovery** is the opposite — **administrative limbo**: no verdict you can read, no reason,
  no operator, no door, a sum that climbs while you press buttons that circle back to the same
  score. It does not *eject* you; it *entangles* you.

One work is being told a lie about yourself; the other is being told *nothing* and not being
released. The no-door is not decoration — it *is* the argument, and it is a felt experience
Native Speaker structurally cannot produce.

Field neighbours: Mimi Onuoha (*The Library of Missing Datasets* — but hers is a contemplative
index of absence; this is a live trap that costs *you* something in real time); Julian Oliver
/ Danja Vasiliev (critical-engineering objects); Hito Steyerl on administrative violence. The
literary shadow is *Der Process* built as a kiosk.

## Spine & tiers

- **SOURCED** — the Dutch childcare-benefits scandal (*toeslagenaffaire*). The studio's own
  research; every load-bearing claim traces to a retrievable primary source (URLs in the
  dossier). Confirmed first-hand this session, twice independently:
  - The tax authorities used **Dutch nationality as a risk factor**; non-Dutch nationals
    received higher risk scores (Amnesty International, *Xenophobic Machines*, index
    EUR 35/4686/2021, Oct 2021).
  - The Dutch Data Protection Authority found this processing of nationality **unlawful and
    discriminatory** (same report).
  - The flag was effectively **unappealable / illegible**: a civil servant reviewing a flagged
    file was *"given no information as to why the system had generated a higher-risk score"* —
    an opaque black box with "an absence of accountability" (same report).
  - Order of magnitude of harm: ~26,000 families wrongly branded fraudsters; repayments
    routinely tens of thousands of euros; the Rutte III government resigned **15 January 2021**.
- **IMAGINED (marked on the work's face)** — the specific on-screen sums shown to *you*
  (dramatized composites), the routing copy, the compounding animation. The kiosk's fiction,
  labelled as fiction, never blended into the SOURCED spine.
- **The landmine the work refuses** — a **child-removal figure will NOT appear on the work's
  face.** The national statistics office could not establish causal linkage (placements rose
  1,115 → 2,090, *associated*, not shown *caused*); asserting causation would be the factual
  error that kills the work the way "The Exemption" was killed. This refusal is binding.

**Climax claim that must hold (verified):** nationality was used as a risk factor, and the
resulting accusation was effectively unappealable — no reason shown, no human reached. Both
documented in the Amnesty primary.

## The takedown & its refutation

**The one-sentence takedown a serious critic would publish:** *"It's Native Speaker with a
benefits form — the studio has one trick, a machine that judges the visitor unjustly, and is
running it again."*

**How the built work refutes it (not merely survives it):** the refutation lives in the
mechanism, not the wall text. Native Speaker's verdict is legible and terminal; Recovery's is
illegible, compounding, and inescapable. The work is not the flag — it is *the loop with no
operator*. The instant this machine puts a legible verdict on its face ("YOU ARE A FRAUD",
or a visible "because you are not Dutch"), it collapses into both didacticism *and* into
Native Speaker, and the studio has copied itself. Refusing to explain itself is
simultaneously the historical truth (parents got no reasons) and the firewall against
self-plagiarism.

**Which of (a)/(b)/(c) the takedown law requires:** **(b)** holds squarely — it implicates
the Dutch state and the live, spreading logic of risk-scoring on protected proxies (SyRI and
its European cousins), power far above the studio. **(c)** holds secondarily — an interactive
machine that *entraps* rather than judges, producing the felt "no-door" that no report can
hand you.

## Binding conditions (from the concept gate — carried into the brief)

1. **No legible verdict, ever.** The machine never tells the visitor *what* it saw or *why*.
   This is at once the historical truth and the firewall against self-plagiarism. The instant
   a legible reason appears on the face, the work dies twice over.
2. **The loop must be genuinely inescapable — and self-evidently so within ~1 minute.** The
   compounding sum must visibly climb; the appeal must genuinely circle back to the same score.
   Real inescapability, not a scripted dead-end labelled "inescapable."
3. **The nationality question is one of several neutral fields and is never revealed as the
   cause.** The visitor may only *suspect*. Confessing the trigger betrays both the source
   (whose engine was silence) and the daylight from Native Speaker.
4. **Keep the IMAGINED marking on the composite sums; keep the child-removal number off the
   face** — as the Artist already insists, and now binding.

## The Kritiker's published critique (ships with the work)

> The failure mode Recovery is one bad build away from is the tasteful diorama — a
> government-grey box that recites a Dutch scandal a Dutch visitor already knows and a foreign
> visitor can't place without a wall label. What saves it from the diorama is precisely what a
> lazy fabricator will destroy first: the illegibility and the no-exit. The instant this
> machine puts a legible verdict on its face — YOU ARE A FRAUD, or a visible "because you are
> not Dutch" — it collapses into both didacticism *and* into Native Speaker, and the studio has
> copied itself. The real scandal's engine was that parents got *no reasons*; the machine's
> cruelty was its silence. If Recovery explains itself, it betrays its source and its sibling
> in the same gesture. The compounding number must genuinely compound; the appeal must
> genuinely circle; the nationality question must sit among innocuous ones and never be
> confessed as the trigger — the visitor may only *suspect*. Build the felt trap, not the
> reenacted anecdote.

## What it needs (physical realisation — a REQUESTS ask when the build approaches)

- One fabricated kiosk: enclosure + touchscreen in government self-service industrial design
  (the ugliness is load-bearing — it must read as *government*, not gallery), plus a **thermal
  printer** so the repayment demand leaves the kiosk as a physical object the visitor carries
  out. A screen-only increment 1 can prove the loop and the mechanism before any fabrication.
- No rights issues (public-record scandal; no personal data collected — the nationality
  "question" is theatrical, nothing stored).
